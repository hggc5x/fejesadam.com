About Me

Hey! Welcome to my gallery. Let me introduce myself. My name is Adam and I'm a part time Photographer and Videographer who enjoying this hobby for a few years already. I take photos not because to earn money, but because I love the process of taking pictures, look people smile and get amazed by the pictures. So if you want some cool photos, hit me up, I'm doing TFCD(Time for photos) photography and professional photography too.
